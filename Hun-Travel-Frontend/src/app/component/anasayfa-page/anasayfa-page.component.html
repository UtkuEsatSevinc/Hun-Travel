About Us

HUN Travel, the leading company in the transportation sector in Turkey, is the largest land transportation company in terms of revenue and number of passengers carried. With a fleet of 1,221 buses, including 495 owned by the company itself, HUN Travel maintains a strong position in the industry.

With a young fleet of vehicles with an average age of 3 years, HUN Travel benefits from the strong capital of its parent company, HUN Holding, and an efficient organizational structure. Additionally, with over 1,000 active agencies spread across 79 provinces in Turkey, HUN Travel has a significant advantage over its competitors with its domestic and international ticket sales network.
